Places To Visit

1. National Museum

Home to the famous fossilized hominid skeleton "Lucy," the National Museum of Ethiopia provides a comprehensive overview of the country's history and culture.

2. Holy Trinity Cathedral

This cathedral is one of the most important and significant religious sites in Addis Ababa. It houses the tombs of Emperor Haile Selassie and his wife.

3. Ethnological Museum

Located on the grounds of Addis Ababa University, this museum offers a fascinating insight into Ethiopia's diverse ethnic groups, their traditions, and customs.

4. Mercato

As one of the largest open-air markets in Africa, Mercato is a bustling and colorful place where you can shop for traditional crafts, spices, textiles, and more.

5. Entoto Park

For panoramic views of Addis Ababa, head to Entoto Hill. It also has historical significance, as it was the site where Emperor Menelik II established his capital before moving it to Addis Ababa.

6. African Union

The African Union Commission is headquartered in Addis Ababa, and its complex is worth a visit for its distinctive architecture and its role in promoting continental cooperation.

7. Addis Ababa Museum

This museum showcases the city's history, from its founding to the present day. It's a great place to learn about the development and growth of Addis Ababa.

8. Red Terror Martyrs' Memorial Museum

Dedicated to the memory of those who suffered during the Red Terror, this museum provides a sobering look at Ethiopia's recent history.

9. Lion of Judah Monument

Located in the heart of Addis Ababa, this monument symbolizes Ethiopia's independence and is an iconic landmark in the city.

10. St. George's Cathedral

Known for its octagonal shape, St. George's Cathedral is an Ethiopian Orthodox church that plays a significant role in the religious and cultural life of the city.

11. Unity Park

Unity Park is an amusement park located in the Arat Kilo district of Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. It was established in October 2019 and is situated within the compound of the National Palace.
